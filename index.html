Hello, I'm Nathan

What is the most important thing we can think about in this most extraordinary moment? — Buckminster Fuller

With near perfect reliability, the smell of fresh coffee in the morning reminds me of this question. Outwardly people describe me as pretty calm and approachable but inwardly I’m a fidgety mess, always trying to understand, solve or figure out where to wander.

Physically, I grew up in Missouri but once the late 90s arrived I was spiritually all over the world, using a version of the internet that required more imagination (and more typing). My friends were bloggers and geeks trying to make sense of a new medium.

I studied Electronic Arts in college which allowed me to explore early forms of online product design, storytelling through filmmaking and 3d animation. California would eventually become my home even though my heart will always long for Kansas.

Nowadays I spend my time building a better future at Facebook. Random thoughts related to the question above find their way to Twitter. Snapshots of adventures show up on Instagram. When I’m building tools for myself I usually commit them to Github. The many books I attempt are logged to Readernaut. And the long runs I take appear on Strava and Ultrasignup.
