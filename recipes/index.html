Recipes

Granola

  • 6 cups oats
  • 2 cup nuts
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tsp ginger
  • 2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup oil (less if using egg white)
  • 1 cup honey

325 degrees for 30-45 minutes. Stir at 20 minutes. Let cool.

Vegan Nachos

  • 2 cups raw cashews
  • 4 cups water (divided)
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons salt, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons Sriracha
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric (for color)

Blend.

Protein Balls

  • 1 cup almonds
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 30 dates
  • 3 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil

Blend. Form into balls.

Lemon Syrup

  • 4 lemons (and zest)
  • 1.25 cups water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Splash vanilla extract

Simmer 15 minutes. 2-3 tablespoons per 8 ounces of seltzer.

Hummus

  • 1 can of chickpeas (15 oz)
  • 1/2 cup of chickpea juice or water
  • 1/3 cup tahini
  • 1 lemon zested
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cloves of roasted garlic

10 minute simmer of chickpeas with 1 teaspoon of baking soda. “Roast” garlic in microwave at 40% power, 5 minutes. Rinse chickpeas and blend everything.

Pour-over Coffee

  1. Rinse filter
  2. +30 grams of coffee
  3. +60 grams of water, wait 30 seconds
  4. +150 grams, wait 60 seconds
  5. +250 grams, 30 seconds
