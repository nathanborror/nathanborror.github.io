Granola

  • 2 cup nuts
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tsp ginger
  • 2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup oil (less if using egg white)
  • 1 cup honey

325 degrees for 30-45 minutes. Stir at 20 minutes. Let cool.

Hummus

  • 1 can chickpeas (15 oz)
  • 1/2 cup chickpea juice or water
  • 1/3 cup tahini
  • 1 lemon zested
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cloves roasted garlic

10 minute simmer of chickpeas with 1 teaspoon of baking soda. “Roast” garlic in microwave at 40% power, 5 minutes. Rinse chickpeas and blend everything.

Pizza Dough

  • 425 grams bread flour
  • 350 grams water
  • 8 grams salt
  • 120 grams sour dough starter

Mix water and flour together, let sit for 10 minutes before adding sourdough starter and salt. Mix for 10 minutes and kneed into a ball. Place in a large bowl coated in olive oil. Let set for 8 hours. Form into ~250 gram balls. Let sit for a couple hours before using or put in fridge.

Vegan Mozzarella

  • 1/2 cup raw cashews (soaked)
  • 1 1/3 cups water
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp tapioca starch

Boil enough water to soak the cashews for 5 minutes (or an hour). Drain cashews and put in blender along with water (not water used for soaking) and other ingredients. Blend and pour watery mixture into a saucepan, bring heat to medium. Stir with spatula until it gets clumpy and then super gooey and stretchy. When it’s one big mass remove from heat ASAP.
