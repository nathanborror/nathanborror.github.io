I also highly recommend Drinks with Lifehacker, those dudes know how to party. Hope to see some of you there. If we've never met don't hesitate to introduce yourself. My [SXSW Flickr stream](http://flickr.com/photos/sketch22/sets/72157594576670321/) should be dripping with nightly photos or join the [Flickr pool](http://www.flickr.com/groups/sxswi2007/).
