../ Lukova Exhibition

Monday, May 10, 2004

Recently Luba Lukova graced our local Art and Design Gallery with some of her current works and sketches. I’ve always been a fan of her style and philosophy. She currently teaches at the School of Visual Arts in Manhattan.

Her unique style focuses on metaphorical references, manipulation of the human form and she uses a very unique treatment of typography. I was able to snap some photos to share with you.
