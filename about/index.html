Just a midwestern kid soaking up the greatest parts of California. If I’m not building something I’m probably on a trail with my partner or preparing for a very long run.

When I am building it’s probably with Go or Swift. I like Go because it’s very stable and backwards compatible, perfect for tools that don’t get touched for long periods of time. Swift because it’s not as boring as Go and I’m usually up for a challenge every summer when Apple decides to change everything.

As for trails and adventuring, I try to keep this wilderness guide up-to-date as I learn what works best for me.
